First offline meet with the seniors. Engineered sand idea was decided as the primary focus.

28/1/2025

The team presented a brief overview of the idea to Ganit and Kaushika. They provided valuable feedback, highlighting key aspects to explore further.

Started uploading iHP content on wiki.

Background, Description, Wet Lab, Dry lab, Hardware and Software and Engineering content was uploaded.

Mock Presentation with all the teams.

9/2/2025

Mock Wiki Freeze

13/2/2025

Final Presentation after Mock Wiki Freeze.
