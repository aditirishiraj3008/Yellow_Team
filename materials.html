B.Growth Curve Analysis

1. Microbial Culture & Storage
• Bacillus licheniformis (stored at 4°C)
• 20% aqueous glycerol solution (for long-term storage at -20°C)
2. Growth Media & Components
Basal Medium:
• Peptone (5 g/L)
• Yeast extract (1 g/L)
• Magnesium sulphate heptahydrate (MgSO₄·7H₂O) (1 g/L)
Fermentation Medium:
• L-glutamic acid (20 g/L)
• Citric acid (12 g/L)
• Glycerol (80 g/L)
• Ammonium chloride (NH₄Cl) (7 g/L)
• Magnesium sulphate heptahydrate (MgSO₄·7H₂O) (0.5 g/L)
• Iron (III) chloride hexahydrate (FeCl₃·6H₂O) (0.04 g/L)
• Dipotassium phosphate (K₂HPO₄) (0.5 g/L)
• Calcium chloride dihydrate (CaCl₂·2H₂O) (0.15 g/L)
3. pH Adjusting Agents:
• 50% NH₄OH
• 3M HCl
4.Glassware & Containers
• 250 mL Erlenmeyer flask (for overnight culture)
• 1 L Flask/fermenter (for batch culture)
• Sterile culture tubes
• 100 mL measuring cylinders
• Pipettes (1 mL, 5 mL, 10 mL)
• Autoclave-safe bottles (for media preparation)
5. Fermentation & Culture Conditions
• Autoclave (121°C, 15 minutes) for sterilizing media
• Water bath (set at 37°C) for thawing frozen cells
• Incubator shaker (37°C, 250 rpm) for overnight culture
6. Microbial Viability Testing
• Nutrient agar plates (for serial dilution & CFU counting)
• Tryptic Soy Agar (TSA) plates
• Sterile dilution tubes (for 10-fold serial dilutions)
• Micropipettes & sterile tips (100 µL, 200 µL, 1000 µL)
• Colony counter (or manual counting)
7. Growth Curve Measurement
• Spectrophotometer (OD600 measurement)
• Cuvettes (for OD readings)
• Sterile 1 mL pipettes (for sample withdrawal)
• Eppendorf tubes (for OD600 measurement samples)
