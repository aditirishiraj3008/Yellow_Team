Hardware

Main hardware involved: Bioreactor

Purpose: Extraction of gamma pga from bacillus subtilis

Type of bioreactor: Modified Simple Stirred Bioreactor

image

Features

  • The fermentation tank lined with stainless steel.
  • Steam inlets and steam outlets.
  • Agitator system consisting of a motor, shaft ,impellers and foam brakers.
  • Temperature, pH,pressure and biosensors.
  • Temperature jacket consisting of fluid inlet and outlet.
  • Sampling port.
  • Sterile air supply.

Working

The bioreactor is first sterilized by passing steam through the inlet provided.Later the innoculators and nutrient medium (after autoclaving) are being fed into the bioreactor by passing it through the nutrient port.Then the rotation is being performed by the agitator system.Proper aeration is also being made sure by the provision given for sterile air supply.Foam brakers are installed to eliminate the froth formation(if it takes place).Temperature and pressure sensors are installed just above the surface of the medium/mixture.pH sensors are also incorporated by making use of probes.The reaction is being performed for various temperature,pressure and ph conditions to optimise the gamma pga yield. The yield concentration is measured using the biosensors.Via the sampling port,the samples of the mixture are taken without disturbing the equilibrium of the system.

Note: The initial experimentations will be carried on in a small scale bioreactor ( Volume: 1-5 litres). Large scale bioreactor implementations will be executed once the temperature,pressure and ph parameters are verified for the maximum gamma pga production.
